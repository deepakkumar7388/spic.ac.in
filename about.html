Our Journey of Excellence

Sardar Patel Inter College was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Koraon Prayagraj. What started as a small school with just 50 students has now grown into one of the most prestigious educational institutions in the region.

Over the past 39 years, we have consistently maintained high standards of academic excellence while nurturing the overall development of our students. Our alumni are spread across the globe, making significant contributions in various fields.

Today, with over 1200 students and 50 dedicated teachers, we continue our mission of shaping young minds and building a better future.

Our Vision

To create a learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and prepares students to become global citizens who can face future challenges with confidence.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, creativity, and moral values, enabling students to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to society.

From the Principal's Desk
