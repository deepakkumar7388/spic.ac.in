Welcome to Our School

Sardar Patel Inter College, established in 1985, is one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Koraon Prayagraj. We provide quality education with modern facilities while maintaining traditional values.

Our mission is to nurture young minds and develop them into responsible citizens who can contribute positively to society.
