Step-by-Step Admission Process

1. Registration

Fill the registration form online or collect from school

2. Interaction

Student-Parent interaction with Principal

3. Verification

Submit & verify all required documents

4. Fee Payment

Pay admission fee to confirm seat

5. Confirmed

Get your child's admission confirmed

Essential Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original + 2 copies)
  • Aadhar Card (Child)
  • Aadhar Card (Parents)
  • Passport size photos (6 copies)
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Address Proof
  • Blood Group Report
  • Medical Certificate
  • Immunization Record
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship)
